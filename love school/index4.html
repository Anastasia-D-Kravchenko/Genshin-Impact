Electro

Electro is one of the seven elements in the game Genshin Impact, which symbolizes the element of lightning.

Charged

When Hydro comes in contact with Electro, it causes the target to become charge The target exposed to it suffers permanent Elemental damage. Charged enemies periodically deal Electro damage to surrounding wet enemies.

Charged Water.

Electro attacks can electrify water. Electrified water deals Electro damage to all creatures in the affected area.

Hydro

Hydro is one of the seven elements in Genshin Impact that symbolizes the element of water.

Hydro Effect

Proximity to water and Hydro attacks impose Hydro effect on creatures. Hydro in contact with other elements can cause an Elemental reaction.

Freeze

The reaction of the Cryo and Hydro elements causes the Frozen status. Frozen creatures cannot move, but they also become as hard as stone.

Pyro

Pyro is one of the seven elements in Genshin Impact that symbolizes the element of fire.

Pyro Effect

Pyro attacks expose the target to the Pyro element. Pyro in contact with other elements can cause an Elemental reaction.

Overload

When Pyro interacts with Electro, an Overload occurs. This status can cause an explosion that deals damage to Pyro. Such an explosion can destroy solid objects with relative ease.

Geo

Geo is one of the seven elements in the game Genshin Impact, which symbolizes the element of earth. This element is also available to the protagonist through the statue of the Seven Archons in Li Yue. To get it, select "Resonate with Geo" in the dialog menu next to the statue.

Crystal

When Geo comes into contact with Hydro/Pyro/Crio/Electro, a Crystal status occurs.
