Kazuha (5★)

A samurai ronin from Inazuma who found refuge in the Southern Cross fleet in Li Yue. This kind and carefree young man has been through a lot in the past.

Skills

< Garju Fencing Style
>

The usual attack:

Up to five quick sword strokes.

Charged Attack:

Two quick swings of the sword, for which a certain amount of stamina is spent.

Falling Attack:

Drops swiftly to the ground, attacking all enemies in its path. Upon landing, deals area damage. If the fall attack was due to the Tihayafuru skill, perform a fall attack: Midare ranzan instead.

Falling Attack: Midare Ranzan

The damage of a fall attack performed thanks to the elemental skill Tihayafuru is converted to Anemo damage. Also, using a arcane technique, this attack creates a small air tunnel that draws nearby objects and enemies into itself.

< Chihayafuru
>

Using an arcane technique as ferocious as a rushing wind, objects and enemies are drawn to Kazuha's current position and thrown into the air in a small radius, dealing Anemo damage . Wind currents also lift Kazuha himself into the air.
Within 10 sec. after lifting Kazuha into the air with Chihayafuru's skill and observing the condition in the air, he can perform a stronger falling attack: Midare Ranzan.

Quick Tap:

Can be done while in the air.

Long press:

Charges for a while and then deals increased Anemo damage over a larger area than a quick press.

Fall Attack: Midare Ranzan

The damage of a falling attack performed with Chihayafuru's elemental skill is converted to Anemo damage . Also using an arcane technique, this attack creates a small air tunnel that draws in nearby objects and enemies. Midare Ranzan's damage counts as falling attack damage.

< Kazuha kick
>

Garyu's special technique is a single swipe, like an approaching storm, inflicting Anemo damage in an area. The trail of the sword leaves behind an Autumn Whirlwind field that deals periodic Anemo AoE damage to enemies within it.

Elemental Absorption:

When in contact with Hydro , Pyro , Cryo , or Electro elements, Autumn Whirlwind deals additional damage with that element. The skill can only absorb one element per use.
